Our Story

Built on responsibility, resilience and commitment.

As a first-born daughter raised between two worlds—home in Zimbabwe and opportunity abroad—I understand the weight of ambition carried by the diaspora. The desire to build, invest, and create impact back home is strong, but it often comes with hesitation: Who can you trust? Who will act with integrity? Who will deliver to the same standard you would if you were there yourself?

Nuvia exists to answer those questions with certainty.

We were created to be a dependable, professional bridge between the diaspora and home—providing trusted property development, construction and student accommodation services rooted in transparency, accountability and excellence. Every project is handled with care, clear communication and the same level of responsibility we would demand for our own families.

Munashe Kurwa
